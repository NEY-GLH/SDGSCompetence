Introduction

This report is a count of the number of staff trained, partially trained or in training for each of the listed competencies.

  • Trained
    The staff member is fully trained in all aspects of the competence and has been signed-off.
  • Partial
    The staff member is trained and signed-off for some aspects of the competence.
  • Training
    The staff member has had this competence assigned and has activtated training but has not yet completed any aspects.
