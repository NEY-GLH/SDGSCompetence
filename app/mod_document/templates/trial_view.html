This report is a count of the number of staff trained, partially trained or in training for each of the listed competencies.

  • Trained
    The staff member is fully trained in all aspects of the competence and has been signed-off.
  • Expired
    The staff member was fully trained but has since expired in at least one part of the competency
  • Partial
    The staff member is trained and signed-off for some aspects of the competence and training is not active for the remainder.
  • In Training
    The staff member has activated training for at least part of this competence.
